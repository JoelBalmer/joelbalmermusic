About Me

I began part-time composing music for media with a short film ‘Alco-hold-up’, screened at the British Film Institute in London in 2010. This page shows a small selection of my work, and my most notable achievements since this first project. In December 2015 I began full-time freelance composing.

Originating from Brighton, England, composing music for media is my passion and my profession. I graduatied from the University of Leeds in 2012 with first class honours in ‘Music, Multimedia and Electronics (BSc)’. I have 10 years experience with Logic Pro. My studio contains a range of instruments I've collected over the years including Guitars, Keyboards, Basses (Double bass), Mandolin, Clarinet and Percussion.
